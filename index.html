About Me

I'm Zane, a filmmaker and videographer with a focus on weddings, short films, and motion graphics. I started working professionally in 2018 while attending film school at UNC Wilmington, and all the work here was produced independently. This portfolio illustrates a variety of skills that are organized around a keen visual sense. My hope is to demonstrate my passion for visuals that evoke emotion, reach a diversity of people, and most of all tell a good story.
